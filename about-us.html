Our Story

"Crafting cozy creations from yarn and heart"


Founded by Sachi, Chi's Crochet began as a simple passion project in 2021. What started as a hobby, crafting common flowers like roses and sunflowers, has blossomed into a flourishing business venture, offering an array of handcrafted treasures.

Sachi's journey with crochet began with a love for the art form and a desire to create beauty with her hands. As she honed her skills and poured her heart into each stitch, she discovered a profound joy in bringing her creations to life. What began as a personal pastime soon evolved into something greater—an opportunity to share her craft with the world.

At Chi's Crochet, we believe in the power of handmade creations to evoke emotion, tell stories, and brighten lives. Every item we craft is infused with care, creativity, and attention to detail. From cozy clothing and stylish hats to elegant bouquets, each piece is a testament to Sachi's dedication and passion for her craft.
What sets Chi's Crochet apart is our commitment to personalized service. We understand that every customer is unique, which is why we offer customized commissions tailored to your individual preferences. Whether you're seeking a special gift for a loved one or a one-of-a-kind piece to elevate your wardrobe, we're here to bring your vision to life.

As a small business, we take pride in our craftsmanship and strive to provide exceptional quality with every creation. When you choose Chi's Crochet, you're not just purchasing a product—you're investing in a piece of artistry, handmade with love and care.

Thank you for visiting Chi's Crochet. We invite you to explore our collection, discover the beauty of handmade craftsmanship, and join us on this creative journey.
